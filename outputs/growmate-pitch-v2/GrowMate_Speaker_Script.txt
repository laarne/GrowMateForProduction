Slide 1: Problem Statement
Before we begin, who here owns a plant, collects plants, or knows a plantito or plantita?
Many people buy and sell plants through Facebook groups, Marketplace, or local sellers. But listings are often unclear.
Buyers may not know the exact plant name, how to care for it, if it is healthy, or if it is safe and legal to sell.
That is the problem GrowMate wants to solve.

Slide 2: Proposed Solution
GrowMate is a mobile plant marketplace and community app for plant lovers, beginners, local sellers, and plantitos and plantitas.
Users can buy and sell plants, showcase their garden, post updates, ask questions, and get support from Leafy AI.
GrowMate is not just a marketplace. It is a safer plant community where users can learn, connect, and grow together.

Slide 3: AI Integration and Technology Used
Introducing Leafy AI, the main AI feature of GrowMate.
When a seller uploads a plant photo, GrowMate can use a plant identification API, such as Plant.id, to identify the plant.
Leafy AI can suggest the plant name, category, care tips, and marketplace description.
It can also help diagnose plant problems and warn users about plants that may be protected, endangered, or risky to sell.
So the AI supports safety, trust, and better plant care.

Slide 4: Target Users and Beneficiaries
Our target users are beginner plant owners, plant collectors, local sellers, backyard gardeners, urban gardeners, and people growing herbs or vegetables at home.
Buyers get clearer and safer listings. Sellers can post faster with AI assistance. Beginners get plant identification and care guidance. Local communities benefit because GrowMate supports small sellers, urban gardening, and responsible plant selling.

Slide 5: Prototype Demonstration
Now, let me show how GrowMate works. In the Market, users can browse plants for sale. When a seller wants to post, they tap Sell Plant, upload a plant photo, and Leafy AI helps identify the plant and check if it may be safe to sell. The seller can add the price, quantity, local plant name, category, and delivery or meetup option. Then the listing can be posted to the Market. GrowMate also has a Feed, Garden, Rankings, and Profile.

Slide 6: Impact
GrowMate creates impact in three ways. First, it makes plant buying safer through clearer listings. Second, it helps beginners learn proper plant care. Third, it supports local plant sellers and gardening communities. It also promotes responsible selling because Leafy AI can help flag risky or protected plants. GrowMate supports SDG 11, SDG 12, and SDG 15.

Slide 7: Scalability and Sustainability
To make GrowMate sustainable, the app can earn through a 10% marketplace fee only when a plant is sold. There is no upfront listing fee, so sellers can post more easily. In the future, GrowMate can add verified seller accounts, premium seller tools, promoted listings, and partnerships. It can also scale to other cities through location-based listings and regional plant communities. Grow smart, grow safe, grow great with GrowMate. Thank you.
